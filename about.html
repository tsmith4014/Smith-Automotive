About Curtis Smith

Curtis Smith, with a rich background in law enforcement, aerospace welding & machining, and a lifelong passion for firearms, now offers his expertise through his gunsmithing services. As an Aerospace Welder, he was qualified to work on precision parts for aviation jet planes, a skill that demands meticulous attention to detail and exacting standards. His unique blend of technical skill and deep respect for the craft of gunsmithing ensures that every project is handled with precision and care.
